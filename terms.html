Booking and Cancellations

Appointments can be booked online or via phone. We value your time and ours; therefore, we request at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Failure to provide notice may result in a cancellation fee.

We strive for perfection in every cut. If you are not satisfied with your service, please let your barber know before leaving the shop so we can make any necessary adjustments.
